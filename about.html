Hello! I am Ziqi Cai, a passionate graphic designer and illustrator originally from China and currently based in Maryland. With a keen eye for detail and a love for telling stories in visual formats, I enjoy transforming ideas into visually elegant and impactful designs. My work spans branding, packaging, illustration, type design, and digital experience.

I am an indoor person who also enjoys outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, and swimming. I once cycled around the Taiwan Island solely for 1,113 kilometers in 12 days.

I believe talent is not just something inborn, but also comes from unceasing efforts and determined goals.
