Hello! I am Ziqi Cai, a passionate graphic designer and illustrator from China and currently based in Maryland. With a keen eye for detail and a love for telling stories in visual formats, I enjoy transforming ideas into visually elegant and impactful designs. My passion for design is sparked by my strong interest in aesthetics, creativity, and craftsmanship. My work reflects a sense of cleanliness, a control of balance, and offers a glimpse of my culture.

With a primary focus on branding and illustration, I also specialize in packaging, type design, motion design, and digital experience. I help personal brands and small businesses find their brand essence and build their identities. Previously, I worked at NetEase Games.

I enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and swimming. A notable adventure of mine was cycling solo around the Taiwan Island, covering a distance of 1,113 km in 12 days. In addition to outdoor pursuits, I also enjoy reading and doodling in my spare time.

I am lucky to have been born with “talent” as my family name in Chinese literally means talent. However, I believe that talent is not solely innate; it also comes from unceasing efforts and determined goals. I am committed to continuing my exploration of the fascinating world of art and design, with my passion and dedication to continual improvement.

For projects, collaborations, and chats, please feel free to reach out at dotzcai@gmail.com.
