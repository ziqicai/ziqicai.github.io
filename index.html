Hello! I’m a passionate graphic designer and illustrator based in Maryland. With a keen eye for detail and a love for telling stories in visual formats, I enjoy transforming ideas into visually elegant and impactful designs. My work spans branding, illustration, packaging, type design, motion design, and digital experience. I previously worked at NetEase Games and earned an MFA in Graphic Design from Boston University. Please feel free to reach out at dotzcai@gmail.com.
