Indian Ceramics Triennale 2024


Common Ground 2024 is the second edition of the Indian Ceramics Triennale, set to take place in New Delhi, India from January to March 2024.

In early January 2023, I had the privilege of collaborating with 200 Million Artisans to design the open call posters for the event. It was an honor to contribute to such a meaningful gathering of artistic talent.

For this project, I drew inspiration from the Triennale’s logo, incorporating its shapes and colors into the poster design. By employing a structured grid system, I organized the textual elements to ensure clarity and visual balance. To infuse the posters with a dynamic energy, I animated the exaggerated color blocks from the logo, creating a captivating flickering effect. Additionally, the Triennale team seamlessly integrated Indian rhythmic music into the background, enhancing the overall visual experience.

I wish Common Ground 2024 a resounding success and I am excited to witness exceptional artists and their works shine on the stage.
